Frequently Asked Questions

Gitopticon uses secure OAuth integrations with Jira and GitHub. You grant read access to your repositories and projects, and we automatically sync your data. Your source code never leaves your Git provider—we only analyze metadata and summaries.

We collect Jira ticket metadata (titles, descriptions, labels, status) and GitHub PR summaries (titles, descriptions, changed files). We do not store your actual source code. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Code scanning tools like Snyk or SonarQube find vulnerabilities in code. Gitopticon is about visibility and governance—helping enablement teams understand what work is happening across the organization, not just what vulnerabilities exist. We're complementary to security scanning, not a replacement.

Setup takes about 15 minutes—connect your Jira and GitHub accounts, configure your interests and rules, and you're done. Most teams start seeing relevant work surfaced within the first day as data syncs.
